# Social Media Do’s and Don’ts

## Do’s
-Maintain respectful and professional communication
-Keep your personal information secure
-Think carefully before posting anything online
-Use strong and unique passwords
-Cross-check information before sharing

## Don’ts
-Avoid sharing sensitive personal details
-Do not post offensive or inappropriate content
-Refrain from spreading misinformation
-Limit sharing your real-time location
-Avoid engaging in unnecessary online conflicts
